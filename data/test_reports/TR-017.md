# Test Report TR-017

Test name: Night Rain Pedestrian Detection

System:
camera_radar_pedestrian_detection

Conditions:
- low illumination
- rain
- reflective road

Results:
- camera-only recall dropped to 0.61
- radar-only recall remained above 0.80
- fusion instability observed in 2 of 10 runs

Conclusion:
Performance degradation is correlated with low-light and reflective road conditions.
